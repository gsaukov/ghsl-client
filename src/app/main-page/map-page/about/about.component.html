About this map.

Hello and welcome to the interactive 2025 population density map!
 This Global Human Settlement Layer, or Population Density Map, is built using data from the European Union's Copernicus Project human-settlement.emergency.copernicus.eu and represents the global human density layer for the year 2025.
Depending on a zoom level population density map uses 3 different by size kind of blocks:
  • 3ss 10000m2 - 100x100 meters rectangle. This level allows you to examine the population density of a street or even a single house.
  • 30ss 1km2 - 1x1 kilometer rectangle. This best represents regional or urban population density.
  • 90ss 9km2 - 3x3 kilometers rectangle. At this zoom level, you can view human presence on a country, continent, or even global scale! Try it in dark mode!

Map offers you following controls:
  • Opacity - Adds transparency to the population density layer, allowing you to view the map alone to find districts, streets, or houses.
  • Interpolation - Smooths transitions between blocks for a harmonized map perception.
  • Dark mode - This is my favorite mode! It emphasizes light green and white blocks, revealing every place of human presence on the map. Its best feature is the ability to create incredibly beautiful map when viewed at the scale of a continent or the whole world!
The farther from the equator

the equator retain their
 
square form. This is

For any questions related to dataset figures please refer to original GHSL source that has clear explanation of data gathering process, calculation methods and analysis techniques per different world region or a country.
Please note that the sum of all blocks on this map, represented as integers, totals to 8,191,966,464 Earth inhabitants.
